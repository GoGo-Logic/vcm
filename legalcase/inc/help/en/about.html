The Legal Case Management system (LCM) is aimed for use by not-for-profit legal advice centres in order to make better follow-ups of their cases, including client consultations, court events and reporting.

Authors

The LCM project was initiated by the Internet Rights Bulgaria Foundation (IRBF). The project was made possible with the financial support of the Open Society Institute (information program) and with the collaboration of the Bulgarian Open Society Institute (law program). Web hosting of the project is provided by Source Forge and by the NGO Bulgaria Gateway.
